Professional Experience

Video Producer at IGN

I worked at IGN for just over two years in San Francisco, California, as a video producer for the news team. I thoroughly enjoyed my role, particularly the opportunities to branch out and edit content beyond news. The team was fantastic to work with, and I valued my time there.

However, the pandemic disrupted our operations, and we all transitioned to working remotely. To my knowledge, we never returned to our original studio, and IGN later acquired a smaller studio post-lockdown.

Transition to IT

After my time at IGN, I sought new opportunities that aligned with my long-standing interest in IT. My first job at 15 was at a PC repair shop in my hometown in Ireland. I repaired customers' laptops, typically handling virus removals and system reinstalls. This early experience sparked my passion for technology and problem-solving.

In my pursuit of an IT career, I am dedicated to expanding my knowledge and skills, particularly in cybersecurity and programming. I continuously seek to learn and grow in this field, driven by my curiosity and desire to help others through technology.
